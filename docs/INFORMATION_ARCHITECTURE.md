# Information Architecture

## Main Navigation

Mandaloria's initial navigation must be clear and with its own identity:

- Home
- Plazas
- Codex Libre
- Holochat
- Clans/Houses
- Members
- Council

Council only appears for users with moderation or administration permissions.

## Home

The home page shows the recent life of the community.

Recommended content:

- recent posts;
- featured debates;
- important announcements;
- new Codex Libre articles;
- clan/house activity;
- new members;
- recommended content for initiates.

## Plazas

Plazas is the Reddit-like area: posts, comments, reactions, and debates.

Initial plazas:

- Central Plaza
- Initiate Questions
- Mandalorian Philosophy
- The Way
- Debates and Discussion
- Lore and Culture
- Creative Forge
- Council Announcements
- Tavern

## Codex Libre

The Codex Libre is the library of essential knowledge of Mandaloria.

Initial sections:

- Fundamental Principles
- Guide for Initiates
- Philosophy
- Culture and Symbols
- History of the Movement
- Rules and Coexistence
- Frequently Asked Questions
- Free Resources
- Manifesto

## Holochat

Holochat is the live conversation area, similar to Discord channels but simpler.

Initial channels:

- welcome
- general
- questions
- philosophy
- library
- announcements
- clans
- projects
- off-topic

## Clans/Houses

Clans/Houses provides social structure and community identity.

Concepts:

- Houses: large, historical, or stable groups.
- Clans: social or thematic groups.
- Circles: study or activity groups.

Examples:

- House of the Archive
- House of the Forge
- House of the Council
- Clan of Initiates
- Circle of Philosophy
- Circle of Guardians

## Council

Council is the administration and moderation area.

Functions:

- user management;
- role management;
- plaza management;
- Codex Libre management;
- reports;
- bans and suspensions;
- moderation actions;
- site configuration.
