# Vision of Mandaloria

## Summary

Mandaloria is a digital community for preserving, debating, and expanding the Mandalorian philosophy.

The central idea is that essential information should be free, free of charge, and accessible. Mandaloria should function as its own place where knowledge is shared without depending on closed platforms.

It is not just a forum. The platform combines three ideas:

- A Reddit-like space for posts, debates, and comments.
- A Discord-like space for live conversation between members.
- A library/wiki for storing knowledge, principles, guides, and important texts.

Mandaloria's own distinction is that these areas form a cycle: a valuable conversation can be proposed, reviewed, and turned into an article in the Codex Libre, preserving sources and recognizing contributions. The community does not just converse; it transforms dialogue into collective memory.

The goal is to create a home of its own for the community, with identity, ranks, clans/houses, moderation, and a clear culture.

## Purpose

Mandaloria exists to bring together people interested in the Mandalorian philosophy and give them a place where they can:

- learn the principles of the community;
- publish reflections, questions, and debates;
- organize knowledge permanently;
- talk with other members;
- belong to clans or houses;
- receive ranks, roles, and badges;
- build a shared community identity.

## Product Principles

- Community before tool: the site should feel like a place one belongs to.
- Permanent knowledge: debates pass, but the library must preserve what is important.
- Provenance: knowledge preserves sources, reviews, and recognized contributions.
- Real responsibility: Houses, ranks, and badges must represent demonstrable contributions or duties.
- Moderation from the start: a public community needs reports, roles, and clear rules.
- Real customization: categories, clans, ranks, permissions, themes, and visual identity must be able to evolve.
- Growth by phases: first a solid base, then chat, advanced clans, reputation, and more complex systems.

## What It Is Not

Mandaloria should not start as a complete clone of XenForo, Reddit, or Discord.

The goal is to take the best of each format:

- from Reddit: posts, comments, votes/reactions, and communities;
- from Discord: channels and community presence;
- from a wiki: organized and curated knowledge;
- from XenForo: permissions, moderation, and serious structure.

## Guiding Phrase

Mandaloria is a community and knowledge network for the Mandalorian philosophy.

## Principle of Freedom

Essential knowledge must be free. The community must be cared for. Freedom requires responsibility.
