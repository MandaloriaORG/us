# Mandaloria

Mandaloria is a digital community that transforms conversation into free knowledge and preserves the identity, responsibility, and memory of its members.

## Community

**Member**:
Person with a community identity within Mandaloria.
_Avoid_: User, account, when referring to the person and not their technical access

**Casa**:
Stable community with identity, responsibilities, and areas of knowledge under its care.
_Avoid_: Group, server

**Clan**:
Social or activity grouping with shared goals and a potentially flexible duration.
_Avoid_: Casa, when there is no permanent community responsibility

**Circle**:
Group focused on studying, researching, or developing a specific topic.
_Avoid_: Channel, team

**Council**:
Set of authorized members to administer Mandaloria and protect its rules.
_Avoid_: Backoffice, superusers

## Identity and trust

**Role**:
Set of capabilities granted to a member to perform specific actions.
_Avoid_: Rank, badge

**Rank**:
Recognition of progression, experience, or community responsibility; does not grant capabilities by itself.
_Avoid_: Role, permission level

**Badge**:
Recognition of a specific achievement or contribution, issued with verifiable provenance and evidence.
_Avoid_: Role, reputation

**Reputation**:
Aggregated history of trust earned through useful contributions and community conduct.
_Avoid_: Popularity, rank

**Archivist**:
Authorized member to care for, review, and maintain Codex Libre content.
_Avoid_: Moderator, generic editor

## Spaces and content

**Plaza**:
Space for lasting publications and debates organized by topic.
_Avoid_: Forum, channel

**Holochat Channel**:
Space for immediate conversation and daily coexistence.
_Avoid_: Plaza, private chat

**Codex Libre**:
Public versioned memory that preserves essential knowledge of Mandaloria.
_Avoid_: Blog, wiki, library, when referring to the product

**Codex Article**:
Reviewed and versioned unit of stable knowledge published in the Codex Libre.
_Avoid_: Post, thread

## Knowledge cycle

**Distillation**:
Community process that transforms a valuable conversation into structured knowledge without hiding its sources.
_Avoid_: Copying, automatically summarizing

**Knowledge Proposal**:
Candidacy to create or improve a Codex article from one or several conversations.
_Avoid_: Draft, report

**Community Source**:
Publication, comment, or message that supports part of a proposal or article.
_Avoid_: External reference

**Recognized Contribution**:
Attributable input that remains associated with an article as part of its provenance.
_Avoid_: Sole authorship

## Community care

**Quarantine**:
Private and reversible state that removes content from circulation while a case is investigated or resolved.
_Avoid_: Deletion, permanent concealment

**Moderation Action**:
Attributable and auditable decision that changes the state or scope of a person or content.
_Avoid_: Silent administrative edit
